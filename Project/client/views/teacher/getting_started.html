Why uploading your documents?
All the documents you upload (tests, quizzes, homework) will be stamped with a unique header above them.
This stamp will ask students to bubble in their id, and it will provide you with a space to grade the document.
Once you upload them again, now graded with the stamp, EasyGrade will automatically update the score of each student, and send a push notification to the student to their phone!
